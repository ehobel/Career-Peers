Mission Statement

We are the gateway to the Career Center. We provide personalized advice through one-on-one, peer-to-peer interactions. We inspire our peers to take manageable next steps towards their professional goals. We create a safe space where our peers are comfortable, their concerns are validated, and their anxieties and feelings are normalized.
